How SIM Registration Works in Pakistan

SIM registration in Pakistan follows a structured process regulated by the Pakistan Telecommunication Authority (PTA). When a user purchases a new SIM card, identity verification is required before activation. The registration process is designed to ensure that each mobile number is linked to a verified identity.

Biometric Verification Process

  1. CNIC verification
  2. Biometric fingerprint authentication
  3. Subscriber registration
  4. SIM activation
  5. Database synchronization

This system helps reduce fraud, unauthorized SIM usage, and identity misuse. Proper SIM registration provides enhanced security, fraud prevention, and accurate subscriber records.

SIM Owner Details refer to information associated with a registered mobile number. Many users search for SIM owner details to verify unknown numbers or confirm subscriber information.
